📡 About This Job

🎯 Technical Police Job — No Graduation Needed

This is a technical constable post in the Bihar Police Radio Organization — the communications wing of Bihar Police. You will handle wireless communication equipment and radio operations. It is a technical government job available right after 12th PCM, without needing a degree — making it one of the best science stream government jobs available at this level.

CSBC Bihar has released Advertisement No. 02/2026 for 993 Constable (Operator) / Sipahi (Prachalak) posts in the Bihar Police Radio Organization. Out of 993 posts, 35% are reserved for women candidates and 2% for Freedom Fighter dependents. This is a permanent state government job with full pension and service benefits.

🏆 Selection Process — 4 Stages

  1. Written Examination (OMR Based) — 100 Marks — Objective type written exam. Subjects: General Knowledge & Current Affairs, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics at 10+2 (Intermediate) level. Negative marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer. Candidates up to 5 times the number of vacancies (category-wise) are shortlisted for PET.
  2. Physical Efficiency Test (PET) — Qualifying Only — Must pass running, high jump, and shot put. No marks are given — it is pass/fail only. Merit is decided entirely by written exam score.
  3. Document Verification — Original certificates for education, age, category, Bihar domicile etc. Failure to produce originals leads to disqualification.
  4. Medical Examination — Final health check as per Bihar Police medical standards before appointment letter is issued.
