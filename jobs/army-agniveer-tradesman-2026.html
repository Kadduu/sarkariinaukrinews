🎖️ About Agniveer Tradesman Post

📋 What Does a Tradesman Do in the Army?

Agniveer Tradesman posts cover a wide range of support roles inside Army units — from cooking, tailoring, and equipment maintenance to driving, IT support, and more. These are the backbone support staff of Indian Army units, ensuring smooth day-to-day operations so that combat soldiers can focus on their primary duties.

The Indian Army has released the Agniveer Recruitment 2026 notification under the Agnipath Scheme with 25,000+ vacancies across all posts. Tradesman is one of the most popular categories — accessible to 8th and 10th pass candidates, with the lowest educational bar of all Army posts.

🏆 Selection Process — 2 Phases

  1. Phase 1 — Online Common Entrance Exam (CEE) — Computer-based test conducted at designated centres. Tentatively scheduled between 1 June and 15 June 2026. Subjects vary by post. For Tradesman: General Knowledge, General Science, Mathematics, and Logical Reasoning. Candidates are shortlisted in proportion to vacancies based on CEE marks.
  2. Phase 2 — Recruitment Rally — Shortlisted candidates attend a Rally at designated venues in their region. Rally includes: Physical Fitness Test (PFT) — 1.6 km run, pull-ups, 9 ft ditch jump, zig-zag balance; Physical Measurement Test (PMT) — height, weight, chest; Medical Examination; and Document Verification. All conducted over consecutive days at the rally site.
