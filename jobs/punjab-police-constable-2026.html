🏆 Selection Process — 3 Stages

  1. Stage 1 — Computer Based Test (CBT) — Two papers on the same day. Paper 1 (100 marks) decides final merit. Paper 2 (50 marks Punjabi language) is qualifying — must score 50% to pass. No interview at any stage.
  2. Stage 2 — Physical Screening Test (PST) & Physical Measurement Test (PMT) — Height, chest, running, high jump and long jump events. Qualifying in nature — pass/fail. Candidates shortlisted from CBT (category-wise) appear for this stage.
  3. Stage 3 — Document Verification (DV) — Original certificates for education, age, Punjabi language, domicile, and category verified. Final appointment based on combined CBT merit and passing all qualifying stages.
